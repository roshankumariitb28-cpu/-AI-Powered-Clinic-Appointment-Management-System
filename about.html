Redefining Comprehensive Healthcare in Sambalpur

Shree Neuro & Dental Health Care is a state-of-the-art diagnostic and clinical treatment center. Our clinic was established with the vision of offering high-level neurosurgical expertise alongside advanced, pain-free dental care.

We focus heavily on precision, using gold-standard sterilization protocols, modern instrumentation, and digital diagnostic models to guarantee optimal recovery pathways for all our patients.
