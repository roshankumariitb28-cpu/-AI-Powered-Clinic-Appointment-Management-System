Home FAQs

What are the clinical consultation timings?

Our clinical hours are Monday through Saturday, 6:00 PM to 9:00 PM. The clinic remains closed on Sundays.

How do I schedule a prior appointment?

Prior appointments can be requested directly by calling our booking line at +91 9337388068 or chatting with us on WhatsApp at +91 7008956183. You can also use our interactive booking form on the Contact page.

Does Dr. Mahesh Kumar Kusta consult at other hospitals?

Yes, Dr. Mahesh Kumar Kusta is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Neurosurgery at **VIMSAR, Burla**. Apart from VIMSAR, he offers private consultations at Shree Neuro & Dental Health Care clinic in Badabazar, Sambalpur during evening hours.

What measures are taken for dental sterilization?

We employ advanced Class-B autoclave vacuum sterilization for all dental handpieces and surgical accessories. Single-use disposables are strictly used where applicable to prevent cross-infections.

Is parking space available at the clinic?

Yes, street parking and clinic front space is available for two-wheelers and passenger vehicles. If you require wheelchair accessibility, please notify our reception desk in advance so we can assist.
